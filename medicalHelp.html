The creators of S.H.E. plea any and all healthcare workers to get in touch with nearby volunteers to help out the women living with life-threatening infections due to poor sanitation.



The procedure is simpler than you might think!
Just register yourself as a volunteer on our app and get in touch with any volunteer around you who will be associated with a nearby group of women devoid of proper healthcare.

Our app will allow you to chat with the said volunteer within the app and you can schedule an appointment at your convenience!

Hats off to you for supporting this noble cause.
